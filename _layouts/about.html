--- layout: default title: About the Researcher --- # About Tommy T. Douglas I am an **independent health researcher** and **patient advocate** based in Missouri. My mission is to bridge the gap between complex clinical research and everyday wellness strategies for seniors. ### My Journey In **2008**, I survived a major heart attack. That experience transformed my life and ignited a passion for understanding the mechanics of metabolic and cardiovascular health. Today, I manage **Type 2 Diabetes** using a combination of Metformin, GLP-1 (Ozempic), and evidence-based nutritional strategies. ### What I Do * **Translate Research:** I break down peer-reviewed studies into clear, actionable insights. * **Advocate for Patients:** I help seniors understand their medications and lab results so they can have better conversations with their doctors. * **Share Freely:** I believe health information should be a **free exchange**. You will never find a paywall on this site. > **Disclaimer:** I am a researcher, not a licensed medical doctor.
